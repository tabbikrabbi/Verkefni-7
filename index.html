History of Drain Gang

Drain Gang is a Swedish rap collective consisting of Bladee, Ecco2K, Thaiboy Digital and Whitearmor. They were formerly associated with the label YEAR0001 and other underground producers like Yung Sherman and Gud.

The group formed in the early 2010s, late 2000s in Stockholm, Sweden and quickly gained a loyal following for their unique production, autotune heavy vocals, and futureistic but old vibe. Their music blends elements of "cloud rap", electronic and experimental sounds. Making them pioneers of hyperpop and alternative rap.

Drain Gang has toured worldwide, collaborating with artists like Yung Lean, Charli XCX and many more, pushing the boundries of modern rap and internet/pop culture.
